My Best Movies

Top Three Movies Right Now

Suzume

This movie was visually stunning! It was a beautiful story and the plot kept me on the edge of my seat.

Oppenheimer

What I loved most about this movie was the music and the sound design. The sound was crisp and almost tactile. It was an immersive experience.

Rango

The style of animation of this film was completely unexpected. I had never seen anything else like it! I loved Johnny Depp's performance as Rango in this movie. I was rooting for him the entire time. I like how Rango learned valuable lessons and showed growth. It reminded me of how we are all trying to figure out who we are and what matters to us.
